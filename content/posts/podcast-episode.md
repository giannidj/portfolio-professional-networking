\# Chat Control Under Fire: Professor Bart Preneel on Privacy, Encryption, and European Surveillance



In this podcast (presented in Dutch), Gianni De Jaeger and Aaron Deceuninck speak with Professor Bart Preneel, head of the COSIC research group at KU Leuven and an internationally recognized expert in cryptography and cybersecurity. They discuss the European “Chat Control” legislation, its impact on privacy and encryption, and why more than 400 researchers warned about the risks of mass digital surveillance in an open letter.
