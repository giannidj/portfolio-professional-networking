# Bachelor Thesis



As part of my Applied Computer Science degree at Howest University of Applied Sciences, I completed my bachelor thesis at PwC Belgium's Forensic Services department.



The project focused on the design and development of a Generative AI-enabled tool that supports investigators throughout the forensic investigation lifecycle. The solution combines Microsoft Power Platform technologies, OpenAI language models and RelativityOne integrations to automate and support several investigation-related activities.



This page contains the final version of the thesis, which can be viewed online or downloaded as a PDF.
